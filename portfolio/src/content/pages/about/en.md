I work on Python backend systems: APIs, services, integrations, data layers, and background processes. What matters to me is not only solving the task quickly, but building a system that can be changed safely after the first release.

In a strong backend system, I value clear responsibility boundaries, explicit contracts, predictable data handling, and maintainability. That is why I pay close attention to architecture, database design, migrations, validation, async flows, and integration quality at the same time.

I am especially drawn to products where the backend is the foundation of the business: internal platforms, API-first services, automation flows, Telegram bots, monoliths with clean structure, and microservices where reliability, observability, and controlled evolution matter every day.
