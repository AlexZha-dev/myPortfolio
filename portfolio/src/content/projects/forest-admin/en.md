Forest Admin is a concept for an administrative workspace used to manage content, showcase visibility, and internal publishing flows.

The main goal here was to make the interface information-dense without making it visually heavy. The design leans on a structured grid, clear hierarchy, and larger functional zones that keep the screen readable.

### What this case highlights

- overview and analytics patterns;
- management screens for cards and publishing;
- a more system-oriented interface compared to landing pages;
- visual discipline in a utility-heavy product.

It is a good example of how I approach admin interfaces: keeping them orderly, readable, and visually composed even when the product is carrying more operational detail.
